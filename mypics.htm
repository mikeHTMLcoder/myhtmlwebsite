A small, 10 gallon Planted Aquarium from my collection.
This is my planted 10 gallon aquarium. That I created out of some free glass that I got off chragslist and spare parts. I had a filter lying around and found the glass rims for free on a curb. Why not set up another aquarium? At the time, I was very interested in nano, or small planted aquariums that are made to just show off the beauty of plants. It is set up and running now and I will be purchasing blue shrimp to keep in it as soon as they are shipped into my local aquarium shop. It has 3 mystery snails that live in it that I got from my other frined in Maine. He is also the one who sent me the driftwood in the aquarium that you can see has moss planted on it. So far I have 6 plant species in this aquarium, but hope to increase it to 8 in the near future once I install a Carbon Dioxide sistem.
