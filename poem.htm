Dear Sarah,

My love for Sarah is so great,
my heart melts for Her 'til the dusk of day.
The night sails when she's away
running, breathes 'til day's dawn.

Her beauty is great,
Wondering mind 'til Her sees,
thinking is all I do,
While waiting for the moment, for Her to say "I do."

Yours truly,

Luke
